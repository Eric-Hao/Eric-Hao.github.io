# Projects
- *2026.03*: Researched, developed, and launched agentic coding LLM, KAT-Coder V2, which ranked 6th globally and 1st among Chinese models on the Artificial Analysis Coding Index (as of March 31, 2026). [![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/mT0GqqLjzWH7sBXavXsFXg)
- *2025.08*: Researched, developed, and launched diffusion-based generative large processing model, which significantly improves video clarity and texture details while preserving fidelity and delivers substantial gains in QoE.
- *2023.01*: Researched, developed, and launched server-side pre-processing algorithms to intelligently reconstruct low-quality videos into high-definition outputs, improving user viewing experience and overall QoE. [![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/gUvhyUOgUavmxEpPXPXcnA)
- *2022.07*: Researched, developed, and launched on-device post-processing algorithms optimized for mobile NPU/GPU chips, balancing model quality, and power efficiency while improving video clarity during content consumption. [![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/c0VxPw3uVoWpelsYtn8wEg)
- *2021.12*: Researched, developed, and launched video quality assessment algorithms for multi-dimensional perception, understanding, and evaluation of video quality. [![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/8rf-Bm918oCF6OGKTuGEUg)

- *2026.03*: 研发上线大语言模型 KAT-Coder V2，在 AA Coding Index 榜单中位列全球第六、国产第一 (截至 26.03.31)。[![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/mT0GqqLjzWH7sBXavXsFXg)
- *2025.08*: 研发上线生成式视频处理大模型 LPM，提升视频清晰度并生成纹理细节，取得了带宽收益与用户时长的双重收益。
- *2023.01*: 研发上线服务端前处理算法，对低质视频进行智能高清重塑，提升用户观看体验和大盘QoE。[![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/gUvhyUOgUavmxEpPXPXcnA)
- *2022.07*: 研发上线移动端后处理算法，提升用户播放视频清晰度，面向NPU/GPU 芯片优化算法，兼顾效果与功耗。[![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/c0VxPw3uVoWpelsYtn8wEg)
- *2021.12*: 研发上线视频质量评估算法，实现对视频画质的多维度感知、理解和评估。[![WX](https://img.shields.io/badge/Post-blue?logo=wechat&logoColor=black&style=social&labelColor=505050)](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/8rf-Bm918oCF6OGKTuGEUg)
